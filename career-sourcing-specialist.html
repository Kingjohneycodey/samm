Sourcing Specialist

Responsibilities:

○ Research and identify potential vendors and manufacturers for fabrics, trims, and other materials.
○ Negotiate pricing and contracts with vendors, ensuring best value for the company.
○ Manage supplier relationships, maintaining communication and ensuring timely deliveries.
○ Source materials that meet quality standards and align with the brand's design aesthetic and budget.
○ Stay up-to-date on industry trends and innovations in materials and production techniques.
○ May travel to factories or trade shows to source materials and build relationships with suppliers.
